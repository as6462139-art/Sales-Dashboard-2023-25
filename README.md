# Sales-Dashboard-2023-25
"Interactive Power BI dashboard tracking sales, profit, quantity &amp; transactions across regions and brands"

🔑 Key Metrics Tracked
Total Sales: 99M
Total Cost: 76M
Total Profit: 23M
Total Quantity: 13K
Total Transactions: 5,095

📌 Features
Category-wise Analysis: Bar chart visualizing total quantity and total sales across product categories (Monitor, Mouse, CPU, Graphic Card, Keyboard, etc.) to identify top and underperforming products.
State-wise Sales Map: Geo-map visual showing the distribution of sales across different states/regions.
Brand-wise Breakdown: Pie chart comparing sales performance across 10+ brands including Samsung, Dell, Nvidia, Acer, Gigabyte, Seagate and more.
Supervisor Performance Panel: Profile-linked cards displaying each supervisor for team-wise sales tracking and accountability.
KPI Cards: Quick-glance cards for Total Sales, Total Cost, Total Quantity, Transactions and Total Profit.
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI Desktop
DAX (for KPI calculations)
Power Query (for data cleaning and transformation)
Interactive slicers and drill-through filters

This dashboard enables quick decision-making by highlighting top-performing product categories, regional sales trends, brand-level performance, and supervisor-wise team contribution — all in a single interactive view.
